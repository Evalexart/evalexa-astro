<article class="legal-block flow">
  <h2>1. Advertising and tracking cookies</h2>
  <p>
    At this stage, the <strong>evalexa.fr</strong> website and its subdomains do
    not use advertising cookies, commercial tracking cookies, or mechanisms
    intended to profile visitors.
  </p>
</article>

<article class="legal-block flow">
  <h2>2. Audience measurement</h2>
  <p>
    At this stage, the website does not use analytics tools requiring tracking
    cookies.
  </p>
  <p>
    If a privacy-respecting audience measurement tool is added later, this page
    will be updated.
  </p>
</article>

<article class="legal-block flow">
  <h2>3. Local preferences</h2>
  <p>
    The website may store certain preferences directly in the user’s browser,
    including the selected language and display theme.
  </p>
  <p>
    These preferences are used only to improve browsing comfort and are not used
    to personally identify the user.
  </p>
</article>

<article class="legal-block flow">
  <h2>4. Deleting preferences</h2>
  <p>
    Users can delete these preferences at any time from their browser settings
    by clearing local data associated with the <strong>evalexa.fr</strong>
    website.
  </p>
</article>
